Access

Please note that due to being situated in an old hay loft, the salon is only accessible via stairs.
If you have mobility issues or any other concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
If there's any way we can help, we will be happy to do so.

Parking

Koyo is situated in the heart of Truro so any car park is suitable. Marks & Spencers and Garras Wharf are the closest.
