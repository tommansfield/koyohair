Dan Blakemore

Dan has been cutting hair since 2000 when he was still in school. Hairdressing has allowed him to travel the world multiple times, from World Hairdressing championships in Europe and the USA to managing salons in New Zealand and Australia. He opened his first salon, 'Tooth & Nail' in Sydney. It was one of the first salons in the area to offer gender-neutral pricing. His second and third salons have both been in Cornwall, UK and also follow the gender-neutral model. Dan is passionate about the craft of hairdressing and after 22 years in the industry, is still eager to learn and share knowledge with others. When you visit a salon that Dan has been involved with creating, you can be sure that every detail of your visit has been paid attention to.
